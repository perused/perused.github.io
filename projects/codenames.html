Codenames Bot

A bot that can play as a player or a spymaster in the boardgame known as Codenames.

Description

By using the cosine similarity between GloVe word vectors, it is possible to gauge how semantically different two words are. Using this notion, it becomes plausible that a computer could calculate cosine similarities to either guess or give a clue about the cards on the table in a game of Codenames. So far, the bot is a pretty good player and a fairly lousy giver of clues. This is because word vectors don't have the capability to capture nuance and also, in my experience, knowing the personality of the person giving the clue is helpful for guessing; the bot doesn't have much of a personality.
